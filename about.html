The studio

A boutique team for once-in-a-lifetime days.

Shutter Light Stories was born from a simple belief — your wedding photographs should feel as considered and curated as the way you designed your celebration.

Led by our principal photographer and creative director, Shutter Light Stories brings together a tight-knit team of photographers, cinematographers and editors who specialise in high-touch, multi-day celebrations.

We work with a limited number of couples each year, allowing us to invest deeply in each story — understanding your families, your rituals and the atmosphere you want to remember.

  • Approach: Editorial, cinematic and quietly luxurious.
  • Clients: Modern couples, public figures, C-suite leaders & creative professionals.
  • Coverage: Christian, Hindu, Muslim & inter-faith weddings, private events and destination celebrations.
